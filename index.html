About YAPPA Foundation

YAPPA Foundation (Yayasan Penyantunan dan Pendidikan Anak Yatim) was established on September 23, 1975, in Denpasar, Bali. Inspired by the values of compassion in Surah Al-Ma’un, the foundation was created to provide care, education, and hope for orphaned and underprivileged children. Initially operating without a dedicated facility, YAPPA began through a family-based care system (non-residential).

Our Vision & Mission

Guided by Islamic principles and values of compassion

Vision

To build a holistic Muslim community that is morally upright, knowledgeable, spiritually and physically balanced, and environmentally responsible.

Mission

  • To establish child care and educational institutions
  • To develop social and economic empowerment programs
  • To provide adequate facilities and infrastructure
  • To build strong partnerships with government, communities, and organizations
  • To expand sustainable and ethical funding sources
