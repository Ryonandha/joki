How to Reach Us

By Public Transport:

You can reach our location using local public transportation or online ride-hailing services available across Denpasar. Simply search for “YAPPA Foundation” or use our address for accurate directions.

By Car:

Our location is accessible by car, and parking is available in front of the building. Please use the designated visitor parking area.

Note:

For visits to our residential facilities (orphanages), we kindly ask you to schedule an appointment in advance for security and coordination purposes.
