Our Programs

Comprehensive support programs designed to nurture, educate, and empower children to reach their full potential

Residential Care

Our residential care program provides a safe, nurturing, and structured environment for orphaned and underprivileged children. Through our orphanages, children receive full support including shelter, nutritious meals, access to formal education, religious guidance, and character development. In addition, we equip them with essential life skills to prepare them for independent living and a better future.

Key Features :

  • Safe housing and full-time care
  • Access to formal education
  • Religious and character development
  • Daily meals and basic needs support
  • Life skills training for independence

Non-Residential Support

This program supports children who remain with their families but need financial and educational assistance, ensuring they can continue their growth without entering residential care.

Key Features :

  • Educational support
  • Living cost assistance
  • Food and basic necessities
  • Family-based care approach
  • Community-based monitoring

Additional Programs

Economic Empowerment

Developing sustainable initiatives to support financial independence and operational stability through cooperatives and livelihood programs.

Key Features:

  • Cooperative (retail & sharia savings/loans)
  • Livelihood programs (e.g., livestock)
  • Income-generating activities

Humanitarian Aid

Providing assistance to communities in need and responding to emergency situations such as natural disasters.

Key Features:

  • Disaster relief support
  • Financial and basic needs assistance
  • Community welfare programs

Partnership Programs

Building collaborations with government, organizations, and private sectors to expand reach and maximize impact.

Key Features:

  • Strategic partnerships
  • Joint programs and initiatives
  • Resource sharing and collaboration
