About YAPPA Foundation

Dedicated to creating brighter futures for orphaned and underprivileged children through compassion, education, and sustainable support.

Our Story

YAPPA Foundation was established on September 23, 1975, in Denpasar, Bali, inspired by the values of compassion in Surah Al-Ma'un. What began as a small initiative by a group of dedicated individuals has grown into a trusted social institution committed to supporting orphaned and underprivileged children.

In its early years, YAPPA operated without a dedicated facility, providing support through a family based care system. Over time, with strong commitment and community trust, the foundation expanded its services by establishing residential orphanages, educational programs, and social initiatives.

For more than five decades, YAPPA has continued to nurture, educate, and empower children helping them build a better future and become independent members of society.

Our Journey

1975

YAPPA Foundation was established, beginning with a family based care system.

1987

Launch of the Boys Orphanage providing full residential care.

1994

Establishment of the Girls Orphanage expanding support.

2005

Formation of cooperative to promote economic empowerment.

Today

YAPPA continues delivering integrated programs across Bali.
